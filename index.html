About Us

PGW (Perfect Glass & Windows Solutions) has been a trusted name in architectural glass and aluminium solutions since 2014. With a strong legacy in delivering precision-crafted installations, we provide premium glass products, high‑performance aluminium systems, and modern automatic entrance solutions. Our expertise extends across residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional projects—ensuring durability, aesthetics, and long‑lasting value.

Our commitment to innovation, quality craftsmanship, and seamless project execution has made us a preferred partner for architects, interior designers, builders, contractors, and homeowners. At PGW, we believe in offering end‑to‑end solutions—from design consultation to installation and after‑service support.
